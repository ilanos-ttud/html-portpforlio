The Best Books According To Sonali

My Top Three Books Of All Time

1. Storm And Silence

Best Female protagonist ever. The book focuses on the condition of women in the late 1880's, it shows how a young lady called as Lillian Linton empowers herself and starts working for a world renold business man Rickard Ambrose.

2. The Guy Next Door

This book is the best teen book ever. Story revolves around a girl called as Clara Wilson and how she becomes better version of herself after the male lead enters her life and challenges her, which improves g=her self confidence.

3. F.A.I.T.H

I Don't know the end of this one but I will surely buy the version. This book opened my eyes about imagination to next extent the way characters are shown and their personalities got me reakky reakkky excited about this book.
